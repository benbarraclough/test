We are One Optiv

“When we feel recognized, represented, and respected, we do our best work and create the greatest value for our clients. That’s why diversity, equity and inclusion is part of our company DNA. From our company values and core competencies, to our employee-led affinity groups and how we think, talk, act and aspire to be, employees with diverse backgrounds, identities, and thoughts provide unique perspectives that help differentiate us as a leading cyber advisory and solutions partner.”

Image

At Optiv, we honor and embrace the diverse perspectives, ideas, backgrounds and experiences of our people. This unwavering commitment is a key element of our company values and core to who we are and how we operate.

When we feel recognized, represented and respected, we do our best work and create the greatest value for our clients. Our approach to diversity, equity and inclusion (DE&I) is grounded in listening, learning and growing with a focus on:

  • Enhancing employee engagement and pride
  • Attracting, developing and retaining talented employees with diverse backgrounds, experiences and identities
  • Expanding our commitment to the communities where we live and work

With steadfast support from leadership, we hold one another accountable for living our company values and strengthening an inclusive and equitable culture, through a variety of companywide programs, practices, policies, trainings, educational forums and structures. Our affinity groups further bring DE&I to life with opportunities for employees to connect, celebrate and share their true selves:

  • Black Employee Network
  • Optiv Women's Network
  • Optiv Pride
  • Optiv Veterans
  • Latino Employee Network
  • Disability Employee Network
  • Asian Employee Network
